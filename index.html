RIDIANTHON: 6-Hour Hackathon Timeline

Registration & Verification

10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Our team and officials will verify participants, confirm team details, and guide you through the initial setup.

Problem Statement Reveal & Instructions

11:00 AM - 11:15 AM

Participants will be introduced to the themes, problem statements, and rules. Be ready to absorb, choose your domain, and prepare your approach.

Hacking Begins - Build Phase

11:15 AM - 4:15 PM

The real action starts! Use your skills and creativity to develop your project. This is your time to design, code, test, and innovate.

Project Submission Window

4:15 PM - 4:45 PM

4:45 PM - 5:30 PM

Shortlisted teams will present their projects in front of the judging panel. Showcase your work, pitch your solution, and answer quick questions.

Results & Prize Distribution

5:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Winners will be announced, certificates distributed, and the event wrapped up with a thank-you note and group photo!
